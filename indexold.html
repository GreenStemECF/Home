About Green STEM ECF

Green Stem Educational and Charitable Foundation promotes research, innovation and commercialisation of research as the sustainable pathway to industrialisation of Nigeria and Africa. We support education, mentorship and green technology solutions that strengthen national productivity and sustainable development.

5Symposium Edition

Mission

To transform research into commercial and industrial impact through strategic funding frameworks, partnerships and capacity building.

Vision

A sustainable African economy powered by research-led industrialisation and inclusive STEM development.

Discover Our Work

Symposium Leadership

Our annual Green STEM Symposium convenes academics, policymakers and innovators to accelerate industrialisation through commercialised research.

Innovation Funding

We advocate for creative funding frameworks, grant support and research-commercialisation models that unlock Nigeria’s industrial potential.

Mentoring Programs

Our mentoring scheme links students and early-career researchers with experienced professionals to build practical skills and green careers.

Sustainable Impact

We prioritize projects that deliver clean energy, agricultural resilience, digital capacity and socially inclusive growth.

Why Green STEM?

By combining science, technology, engineering and mathematics with environmental stewardship, we create solutions that are economically viable, socially equitable and climate-responsive.

Our Approach

We connect research institutions, government agencies and companies through collaborative networks, shared funding models and commercialization pathways.

Support Our Mission

Donations help us expand mentorship, research support and green innovation programs across Nigeria.
