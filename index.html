Who We Are

Green Stem Educational and Charitable Foundation

We focus on advancing education, research, and innovation by integrating environmental sustainability with science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

Green Stem fosters development and provides resources, mentoring, and opportunities for young people to solve real-world problems. The foundation emphasizes building capacity, promoting underrepresented groups, and enhancing STEM career pathways.

What We Do

Discover Our Initiatives

Through initiatives like the Green STEM Symposium, we promote research and industrialisation, with a focus on technical skills development and academic opportunities.

Green Stem Symposium

Our maiden Symposium in 2022 explored rapid industrialisation in Nigeria. In 2023, we examined the Trilogy of Industrialisation — Government, Research Institutions, and Companies. In 2024, the theme became "Industrialisation as the Best Systemic Strategy to Solve Nigeria's Socioeconomic Problems".
